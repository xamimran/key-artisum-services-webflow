Why Partner With Us

Trusted by Families and Referral Partners

When children need autism services, choosing the right provider matters. At Key Autism Services, we partner with pediatricians, schools, therapists, early intervention programs, daycare providers, and community organizations to help children receive high-quality ABA therapy.

Our experienced clinical team makes the referral process simple while keeping families informed every step of the way. Whether services take place in the home, clinic, school, or early intervention setting, our goal remains the same: helping children reach their fullest potential.

That's the Key Difference.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I refer a child?+–
Simply complete the referral form below or call our intake team directly. We'll take it from there.
How long does the referral process take?+–
Most families are contacted within 1–2 business days of a referral being submitted.
Do you accept my insurance?+–
We work with most major insurance carriers, including Medicaid, Aetna, Cigna, Optum, Blue Cross Blue Shield, and Humana. View our full list of accepted insurance plans.
Where do you provide services?+–
We currently provide ABA therapy in Alabama, Colorado, Georgia, Illinois, Massachusetts, North Carolina, Tennessee, and Texas.
What ages do you serve?+–
Our clinical team supports children from early intervention age through adolescence.
Do you provide clinic-based ABA?+–
Yes, clinic-based ABA therapy is available at our locations across the states we serve.
Do you offer in-home ABA?+–
Yes, our clinicians provide in-home ABA therapy tailored to each family's routine and environment.
Do you provide school-based ABA?+–
Yes, our school-based ABA therapy program delivers support directly in classrooms and school settings where challenges arise.
How quickly will someone contact the family?+–
Our intake team aims to reach every referred family within 1–2 business days.
